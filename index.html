About Us

Romanza fashion is a women clothing and designer store that lies in the heart of beautiful town Payyanur. The mixture of stylish, cool, and in fashion clothing made Romanza fashion an attractive and spectacular five and dime. Hiking in to shop you will be attended by pleasant and patient staffs for assisting you in finding a special look or you just want to check over your own.

What is Our Goal?

Moly AM and Sibi Chacko the owners opened Romanza Fashion in 2017 July. The inauguration was done by panchayath president. They have an experience of more than 10 years in fashion field. Their vision of providing excellent customer service and the latest in fashion to women of all ages has made Romanza Fashion a favorite among shoppers.

  • The quality of material produced and distributed is assured and guaranteed. The materials are imported from Ahmadabad the city known for producing textiles. The manufactures provides a wide range of collection under cotton, silk, rayon, georgette and cotta silk materials.
  • It provide the customers both wholesale and retail services. The materials are presented to the customers according to their needs.
  • Kurthies, Long gown, Skirt, Skirt top, Jean top, Party wear gown are the main attraction. The team also provide the customers with bulk materials for framing dress code for functions.
  • Romanza Fashion is known for the designing of better quality products with affordable cost.
